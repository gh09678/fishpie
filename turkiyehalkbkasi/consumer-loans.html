Turkiye Halk Bankasi A.S. Loans & Credit Services

Consumer Loans

 

Signature Loans

 

For those of you with established credit and good credit scores, we offer signature loans. These are designed to help you with small purchases - such as furniture, appliances, or to just help out on special occasions, with no collateral required other than your good name.


Personal Line of Credit

This is the same as the signature loan mentioned above except we give you an established line that you can use at your convenience. Works much the same as a credit card, except you can only draw from the Personal Line of Credit by visiting a teller, or transferring to another account via Online Banking, and you don't have any plastic to take up space in your wallet. Pay finance charge only on the balance used and we'll be glad to set it up for repayment through payroll deduction.


Vacation Loans

Once winter if finally over, our thoughts turn to warm weather and summer vacation.
Do you have someplace special you would like to visit with your family or friends but don't have the cash? Turkiye Halk Bankasi A.S. can help.
Stop into one of our offices to apply for a Vacation Loan, today!

Loans are available for $2,500 - $5,000 with a reduced processing fee and repayment terms up to 18 months.
