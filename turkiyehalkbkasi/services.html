:: DIGITAL WALLET ::

Paying is even easier when you add your Turkiye Halk Bankasi A.S. debit card or credit card to your digital wallet. Our cards are compatible with Google Pay™, Apple Pay® and Samsung Pay®, giving you the flexibility to make in-store purchases without taking your plastic card out of your wallet. Save time and have peace of mind that your card information is secure. Payment details are never stored on your device or shared with merchants. Instead, a one-time-use number is generated each time a payment is initiated.
