VISA Credit Cards

 

Turkiye Halk Bankasi A.S. has plastics you can use to pay for something now from your checking account and plastics to pay for something later. Our plastics make your life easier and more convenient! Credit cards are everywhere and accepted by virtually everyone. Today, credit cards are sold by everyone, too! Don’t get fooled by the big card companies with their slick marketing and introductory rates. Our cards are easy to understand and we’re here, in person, if you have a question.

 

 

Regular Visa or MasterCard

Fixed interest rate of 13.92% APR*.  A great "starter" card for students, those new to credit or rebuilding credit.

 

Platinum Visa

Fixed interest rate as low as 9.99% APR* (Based on credit qualifications).  Perfect for those with larger credit line needs and established credit.

 

Features of both cards:

  • No annual fees
  • No cash advance fees
  • No balance transfer fees
  • No hidden rate increases
  • No over limit fees
  • Protection with Verified by Visa
  • The convenience of local service from HALKBANK employees
  • Make payments through payroll deduction, Online Banking, Mobile Banking, online at EZCardInfo.com, at any of our teller windows or by mail

Payments can be made right up until the due date at any of our teller windows. Simple, convenient and practical!
